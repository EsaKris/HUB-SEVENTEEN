OUR MISSION & VISION
Shaping Sustainable Futures

Pioneering innovation and collaboration to accelerate progress toward the Sustainable Development Goals through strategic partnerships and community empowerment.

Hub 17 is an innovation and co-creation center dedicated to addressing the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) through purpose-fit initiatives by providing a platform for ideation, co-creation, development, and deployment of solutions through a Strategic Doing framework.

Our Mission

To establish a dynamic and inclusive platform that encourages the development of innovative solutions, nurtures partnerships, and empowers communities to address the SDGs effectively.

Our Vision

To become a global model for fostering innovation, collaboration, and sustainable development, driving the realization of the SDGs and charting a path for their continued achievement beyond 2030.

Hub 17's Purpose & Impact

Transforming ideas into sustainable impact through strategic collaboration

Hub 17 serves as an innovation and co-creation center dedicated to addressing the Sustainable Development Goals through purpose-fit initiatives. We provide a comprehensive platform for ideation, co-creation, development, and deployment of solutions through a Strategic Doing framework.

Inspired by the critical juncture of the SDG halfway mark, we accelerate action by harnessing local resources to solve problems and create lasting value. Our center serves as both a melting pot and thought leader for research, development, partnership building, and visionary planning.

Beyond 2030, we're already charting the path for continued sustainable development, bringing together diverse stakeholders to drive meaningful change.
