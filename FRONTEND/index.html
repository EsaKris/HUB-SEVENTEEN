About HUB 17

We're an innovation space dedicated to tackling the Sustainable Development Goals through practical, community-driven solutions. Our focus is on creating real impact where it matters most.

Based across Africa with global partnerships, we bridge innovation with implementation, turning ideas into action for sustainable development.
